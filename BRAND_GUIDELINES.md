# Intervuu Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview

**Intervuu** is a comprehensive interview preparation and hiring platform that connects three key parties:
- **Job Seekers** - Preparing for and landing their dream jobs
- **Interview Coaches** - Expert interviewers providing mock interview practice
- **Recruiters** - Companies hiring top talent

Our platform takes candidates from **preparation to placement** through mock interviews, job applications, and recruiter connections.

---

## Logo

### Primary Logo (Logo 2 - Three-Sided Marketplace)

The Intervuu logo represents our three-sided marketplace ecosystem:

**Symbol Elements:**
- **Top Circle (Green)**: Talent/Job Seekers - Upward arrow symbolizing career growth
- **Bottom Left Circle (Blue)**: Mock Interview/Practice - Speech bubbles representing conversation
- **Bottom Right Circle (Green)**: Interview Success - Checkmark symbolizing achievement  
- **Center Circle (Orange)**: Intervuu Platform - Connecting all parties together

**Design Rationale:**
- Triangle structure shows stable, interconnected ecosystem
- Three nodes represent the three parties we connect
- Center orange circle emphasizes the platform as the connector
- Flowing lines show the journey from practice to success

### Logo Variants

1. **Full Logo** - Icon with labels + "Intervuu" wordmark
   - Use for: Website hero, marketing materials, presentations
   - Minimum size: 180px width

2. **Horizontal Logo** - Simplified icon + wordmark side-by-side
   - Use for: Website headers, email signatures, business cards
   - Minimum size: 120px width

3. **Icon Only** - Triangle structure without text
   - Use for: App icons, favicons, social media profiles, small spaces
   - Minimum size: 32px √ó 32px

### Logo Usage

**Do:**
- Maintain clear space around logo (minimum 20px)
- Use on white, light gray, or gradient backgrounds
- Scale proportionally
- Use approved color variants

**Don't:**
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Change colors arbitrarily
- Add effects (shadows, glows, etc.)
- Place on busy backgrounds
- Rotate or flip the logo

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice

**Professional yet Approachable**
- Speak with expertise but remain accessible
- Avoid jargon unless necessary
- Be encouraging and supportive

**Empowering & Action-Oriented**
- Focus on outcomes and transformation
- Use active voice
- Emphasize user control and success

**Clear & Concise**
- Get to the point quickly
- Use bullet points and short paragraphs
- Make CTAs obvious
